FAQ

What people need to know before they jump in.

Do I need my own OpenClaw setup?

Yes. The Construct connects to your existing OpenClaw gateway. It doesn't include LLM access — you bring your own Claude Max subscription, API keys, or other model providers.

Can I use The Construct with non-Claude models?

Yes. Claude models route through the CLI binary. Non-Claude models route through the OpenClaw gateway. Both work seamlessly.

What happens when my free sessions fill up?

Your oldest sessions are archived, not deleted. You can upgrade to Red Pill for unlimited history, or manually clear sessions to free up space.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No contracts. Cancel from your account and you'll revert to Blue Pill at the end of your billing period. Your session data stays — nothing is deleted.

Is there a student or open-source discount?

Email us. We're happy to work something out for students and active open-source contributors.
