The Construct: Privacy Policy

Effective: July 1, 2026.

This Privacy Policy explains how AcerLogic Educational Services, LLC ("AcerLogic," "we," "us") handles information in connection with The Construct application (the "App") and the website at theconstructapp.com (the "Site"). It is part of, and incorporated into, our Terms of Service.

The short version

The Construct is local-first and bring-your-own-key. Your projects, conversations, files, and AI credentials live on your own Mac. We generally do not receive your content. A few specific things leave your device, described below, and we tell you what and when. We do not bundle analytics or tracking SDKs in the App, and we do not sell your personal information.

1. Information stored on your device (we do not receive it)

The App stores the following locally on your Mac, in your user folders, and AcerLogic does not collect or have access to it:

  • Your projects, threads, messages, and Output.
  • Your Agents' working files and the App's automatic backups.
  • Your AI Provider credentials, which are encrypted at rest (AES-256-GCM) and transmitted only to the provider they belong to.
  • The name and settings you enter, which personalize the App locally.

2. Information that leaves your device, and to whom

  • Your AI Providers. To do work, the App sends your prompts, instructions, and the files you choose to whichever AI Provider you connect (for example Anthropic, OpenAI, or Google). That information is handled under the provider's privacy policy, not ours. If you use a local model runner, this content does not leave your Mac.
  • Voice synthesis (only if you enable voice output). If the App speaks replies using the included voice service (Cartesia) or ElevenLabs, the text to be spoken is sent to that service to generate audio. Turning your speech into text runs locally on your Mac and is not transmitted. The built-in macOS voice is fully local.
  • Update checks. The App periodically checks theconstructapp.com for a newer version. This request is to retrieve a version manifest and does not include your content; like any internet request, it involves your IP address and basic connection metadata at the time of the check.
  • Phone intake (off by default). If you enable answering by iMessage or Telegram, the relevant messages are processed on your Mac; Telegram messages transit Telegram's service (api.telegram.org) under Telegram's terms. This feature is disabled unless you turn it on.
  • Payments. If you purchase a license, payment is processed by Stripe. Stripe collects your payment details and email under Stripe's privacy policy. We receive a record of the purchase (such as your email and that a payment was made); we do not receive or store full payment-card numbers.
  • Support. If you contact us (for example by email), we receive what you send us (such as your email address and message).

3. The Site (theconstructapp.com)

The Site is a marketing and download site. When you visit it:

  • Hosting logs. Our host records standard server-log data, including IP address and request metadata.
  • Analytics (Plausible). We use Plausible Analytics, a privacy-focused, cookieless analytics tool, to understand aggregate traffic and which links are used. Plausible does not set cookies, does not collect personally identifying information, and does not track you across other sites.
  • Fonts and assets (Google Fonts; CDN). The Site loads web fonts from Google Fonts and styling assets from a content-delivery network. To serve those assets, those providers receive your IP address.
  • Email signup (Buttondown). If you submit your email address to our signup form, it is sent to and stored by Buttondown, our email provider, so we can contact you about The Construct. You can unsubscribe at any time.

Because our analytics is cookieless and the Site does not set advertising cookies, the Site does not present a cookie-consent banner; the third parties above are disclosed here instead.

4. How we use information

We use the limited information we receive to: provide and maintain the App and Site; deliver updates; process and support purchases; respond to your requests; protect against fraud, abuse, and security issues; and comply with law. We do not use your content to train models, and we do not sell or rent personal information.

5. Sharing

We share information only as described here: with service providers that help us operate, under terms limiting their use, including Stripe (payment processing), our website host (static hosting and server logs), Plausible (cookieless website analytics), Google Fonts and a CDN (serving website assets), and Buttondown (email signups); to comply with law or valid legal process; to protect rights, safety, and security; and in connection with a business transfer (merger, acquisition, or sale of assets), in which case we will seek to ensure the recipient honors this Policy. We do not control, and are not responsible for, the privacy practices of AI Providers or other third-party services you connect.

6. Security

We design the App to keep your data on your device and to encrypt your AI credentials at rest. No method of storage or transmission is perfectly secure, and you are responsible for securing your own device, accounts, and credentials.

7. Retention; deletion

Your local data persists on your Mac until you delete it. Because it is local, you control retention: you can delete projects in the App, and you can remove the App's data folders. The App's "export your data" feature reports where your data lives so you can copy or delete it. We retain the limited records we hold (such as purchase records and support correspondence) as needed for the purposes above and as required by law.

8. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or port personal information, to opt out of certain processing, and to be free from discrimination for exercising these rights. Because most of your information is local and within your control, many of these rights you can exercise yourself directly in the App. For information we hold, contact info@theconstructapp.com and we will respond as required by law. We do not sell or "share" personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising as those terms are used under California law.

California (CCPA/CPRA). California residents have the rights described above. The categories of personal information we may receive are identifiers (such as email) and commercial information (such as a purchase record). We do not sell or share personal information.

EEA/UK (GDPR/UK GDPR). The App is offered from the United States and is intended for U.S. users. Where the GDPR or UK GDPR nonetheless applies to you, our legal bases are performance of a contract (providing the App and processing purchases), legitimate interests (security and product operation), and consent (where required, such as for optional features). You have rights of access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and objection, and may lodge a complaint with your supervisory authority. Contact info@theconstructapp.com.

9. Children

The App is not directed to, and is not intended for, children under 18, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

10. International users

We operate from the United States. If you use the App from outside the U.S., you understand that the limited information we receive is processed in the U.S.

11. Changes

We may update this Policy. We will post the updated version with a new date and, for material changes, provide additional notice. Continued use after changes take effect constitutes acceptance.
